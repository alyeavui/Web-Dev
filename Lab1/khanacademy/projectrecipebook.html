Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Chocolate chips 2 cups

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2: Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in one bowl. Cream butter and sugar in another bowl. Combine both mixtures and add chocolate chips.

Step 3: Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes.

Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g

Step 1: Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water.

Step 2: Fry pancetta until crispy. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.

Step 3: Toss pasta with pancetta and egg mixture off heat. Add pasta water if needed. Serve immediately.

Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup

Step 1: Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. In another bowl, whisk milk and eggs together.

Step 2: Combine wet and dry ingredients. Heat a non-stick pan and pour batter to form pancakes.

Step 3: Cook until bubbles form, then flip. Serve warm with syrup or toppings.
